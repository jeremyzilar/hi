# Big Questions

These are the fundamental questions we need to answer to determine if and how to proceed with this gathering.

## Cost & Logistics

### What would it cost to do it at Ghost Ranch?

- Total venue costs
- Per-person costs for lodging
- Meal costs
- Facility rental fees
- Any additional amenities or services
- Average transportation costs (car rental, shuttle van)

### What is the right number of people?

- Minimum viable group size for meaningful conversation
- Maximum size before losing intimacy
- Sweet spot for group dynamics
- Sweet spot for cost at Ghost Ranch
- Consider: High Ground model assumes people know each other well

### What is the right number of days?

- Minimum to make the trip worthwhile (3-4 days)
- Maximum before fatigue/diminishing returns (5 days max)
- Balance between travel time and event duration
- Time needed for organic schedule assembly

### What resources does Ghost Ranch provide?

- Lodging capacity and types
- Food service capabilities
- Meeting spaces (indoor)
- Outdoor spaces
- Audio/visual equipment needs
- Other amenities

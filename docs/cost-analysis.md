# Cost Analysis & Sweet Spot Calculator

Analysis to determine the optimal number of people and duration for a gathering at Ghost Ranch.

## Key Variables

1. **Number of attendees** (8, 12, 15, 20, 25, 30)
2. **Number of nights** (2, 3, 4)
3. **Lodging tier** (dorm-style, mixed, private)
4. **Meeting space needed** (based on group size)

## Quick Cost Components

### Per-Person Daily Costs
- **Meals**: $45.75/day (fixed)
- **Lodging**: $120-$235/night (variable by choice)
- **Meeting space**: Amortized across group

### Fixed Costs (Independent of Group Size)
- Meeting room rental: $315-$600/day depending on size
- Any special venue rentals

### The Sweet Spot Question

What combination gives us:
- ✅ Intimate enough for High Ground format
- ✅ Enough people for diverse conversations
- ✅ Worth the travel/time investment
- ✅ Financially reasonable per person
- ✅ Manageable total budget

## Other Cost Considerations

### Travel Costs (Not Included Above)
- Flights to Albuquerque: $200-600 depending on origin
- Car rental + gas: $150-300 (could split among carpoolers)
- **Estimated additional**: $350-900/person

**Total all-in per person**: $1,130-1,680

### Potential Cost Reduction Strategies

1. **Dorm-style lodging** for those comfortable
   - Saves ~$50/person/night
   - For 15 people, 3 nights: Save ~$2,250 total

2. **Skip some meals**
   - Breakfast: Easy to DIY ($15.25 savings/person/day)
   - For 15 people, 3 days: Save ~$687

3. **Use free outdoor spaces**
   - Labyrinth, Alfalfa Field
   - Save meeting room costs ($1,080 for 3 days)

4. **Casa del Sol rental** (if 15 people)
   - $2,000/night × 3 = $6,000 total lodging
   - vs. $7,875 for mixed individual rooms
   - Save $1,875 + included meeting space

### Potential Cost Increase Factors

1. **All private rooms**: +$50-60/person/night
2. **4 nights instead of 3**: +$220/person
3. **Larger meeting space**: +$240-840 total
4. **Special activities**: Variable
5. **Alcohol/special provisions**: Variable

## The Real Question: What's Worth It?

For attendees to invest:
- **Time**: 4-5 days including travel
- **Money**: $1,200-1,700 all-in
- **Attention**: Full presence required

The gathering needs to deliver:
- ✅ Conversations they can't have elsewhere
- ✅ Connections worth maintaining
- ✅ Insights worth the investment
- ✅ Experience worth repeating

**Threshold**: Probably 15+ people and 3+ nights to justify the investment

## Recommendation

This provides:
- 🎯 Optimal cost-per-person (~$770-780)

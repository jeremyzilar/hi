# Ghost Ranch Information

Comprehensive venue details for Ghost Ranch in Abiquiú, New Mexico.

## Venue Overview

Ghost Ranch offers indoor and outdoor spaces for group retreats, conferences, workshops, and events in a dramatic high-desert landscape with iconic views of Chimney Rock, Pedernal, and surrounding red cliffs.

**Total Capacity:**
- Approximately 100 rooms
- Multiple meeting spaces (15-175 capacity)
- On-site dining for all meals
- Camping and RV options available

## Meals

All meals served in Ghost Ranch dining hall.

**Pricing per person:**
- Breakfast (7:30-8:30am): **$15.25**
- Lunch (12:00-1:00pm): **$15.25**
- Dinner (5:30-6:30pm): **$15.25**

**Total:** $45.75 per person per day for all three meals

**Notes:**
- Children under 12: Half-price
- Children under 5: Free
- Groups can choose which meals to include
